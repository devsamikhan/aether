# AETHER Project Governance

## Overview
AETHER is maintained by a core steering committee and designed via open RFCs.

## Steering Committee
- **Lead Architect**: Samik Khan (@devsamikhan)
- **Core Maintainers**: AETHER Core Team

## Decision Process
1. **RFC Submission**: Major architectural changes must go through the Request for Comments (RFC) repo.
2. **Review Period**: Community discussion is open for at least 14 days.
3. **Core Approval**: Consensus of the Steering Committee is required to merge and implement.
