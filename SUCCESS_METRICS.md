## 📈 Key Performance Indicators

- **Test Coverage**: Maintain automated test coverage above **90%**.
- **JIT Compilation Speed**: Target compilation speeds under **50ms** for average project modules.
- **Runtime Simulation Performance**: Minimize memory footprints of speculative timeline graphs under **2MB** per timeline fork.
- **Diagnostics Accuracy**: Target **100%** span coverage for parser syntax errors.

### 2. Community Metrics
- **GitHub Engagement**: Target **1,000+ stars** and **50+ forks** within the first 6 months.
- **Active Contributors**: Reach **20+ external contributors** submitting Pull Requests.
- **Issue Resolution Rate**: Maintain average issue triage and response times under **24 hours**.

### 3. Adoption Metrics
- **Package Registrations**: Support **100+ community libraries** registered on Aether index indexes.
- **Local Installs**: Reach **5,000+ monthly unique installation downloads**.
- **Run Times**: BCI and Quantum simulators utilized across academic research papers.
